Meet the Beloved Andrea Bocelli

Source: @ab58fan via Flickr.

Andrea Bocelli is considered to be "the world's most beloved tenor." He is a world-renowned Italian opera singer, songwriter and producer with fifteen studio albums created to date. Aside from classical music and opera, Bocelli has crossed over into pop music. Many of his most famous collaborations include creating music with Celine Dion, Jennifer Lopez, Ed Sheeran and more. Bocelli has sold over 90 million records around the world enchanting generations with his beautiful vocal masterpieces. Celine Dion is known to have described Bocelli's voice by saying, "if God would have a singing voice, he must sound a lot like Andrea Bocelli."
